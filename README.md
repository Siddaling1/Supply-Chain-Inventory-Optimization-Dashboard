# Supply-Chain-Inventory-Optimization-Dashboard

## Overview
This project is an interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze and optimize supply chain performance. It helps track key business metrics such as total revenue, stock levels, shipping costs, defect costs, manufacturing costs, and supplier lead times.

The dashboard provides valuable insights into inventory management, supplier performance, and logistics efficiency.

---

## Objectives
- Monitor overall supply chain performance
- Track inventory stock levels across products
- Analyze revenue contribution by city
- Evaluate shipping costs by transportation mode
- Measure supplier lead time and defect costs
- Identify inventory risks and operational inefficiencies

---

- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Shipping Cost
- Average Defect Cost
- Manufacturing Cost
- Average Lead Time

## Dashboard Insights
### 1. Shipping Cost Analysis
Compares shipping costs across different transport modes:
- Road
- Air
- Rail
- Sea

### 2. Supplier Lead Time Analysis
Tracks lead time performance of suppliers to identify delays.

### 3. Revenue Contribution by City
Shows revenue distribution across:
- Mumbai
- Kolkata
- Delhi
- Chennai
- Bangalore

### 4. Product Revenue Analysis
Analyzes revenue generated by:
- Skincare
- Haircare
- Cosmetics

### 5. Stock Level by Product
Monitors product stock availability for inventory optimization.

### 6. Defect Cost by Supplier
Evaluates supplier quality based on defect cost.
